# Background

This repository is the continuation of prior Speaker Mining work and aims to consolidate it into one open implementation.

## Major Predecessors

1. LanzMining by arrrrrmin (initial practical Speaker Mining approach):
   https://lanz-mining.arrrrrmin.dev/
2. Lanz Mining but FAIR by Omar Imad Remmo (extended method and implementation):
   https://github.com/omarrmo/Lanz-Mining-but-Fair
   Thesis DOI: https://doi.org/10.15488/20751
